User Guide

Welcome to tank tastic.
Tank Tastic is a physics based, 2D battle game in which you must use the 
weapons at your disposal to defeat your enemy before they do the very same thing to you.

Use the right arrow key to move your barrel to the right.
USe the left arrow key to move your barrel to the left.
Use the up arrow key to increase the power of your shot.
Use the down arrow key to deacrease the power of your shot.
Use spacebar to fire.
Clicking on the weapons tab will open your list of weapons, click one to equip it.
